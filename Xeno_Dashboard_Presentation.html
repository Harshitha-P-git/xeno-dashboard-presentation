ğŸ’¡Key Design Decisions

Why These Metrics?

Active Campaigns, Today's Reach, Revenue Impact, and Engagement Rate are actionable and directly tied to marketer goals. Revenue connects efforts to business outcomes.

Why Show Active Campaigns?

Marketers need to monitor running campaigns in real-time. This prevents issues from going unnoticed and enables quick optimization.

Why Quick Actions?

Most marketers come to create campaigns or offers. Making these immediately accessible reduces clicks and improves workflow efficiency.

Why Alert Banner?

Critical issues need immediate attention. A banner ensures visibility without requiring navigation, preventing costly mistakes.
